This page now hosts a Number Guessing Game. The player is prompted to guess a number between 1 and 100. After submitting their guess, the game checks if it matches the randomly generated number. If the guess is correct, the player wins; otherwise, they are prompted to try again. The game also provides feedback on whether the guess was too low or too high.
