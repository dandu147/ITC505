Welcome to the custom font- ROBOTO

Research analysis on "Roboto" font:

  1. Roboto font was designed entirely in-house by Christian Robertson.
  2. This font was officially made available for free download on January 12, 2012, on the newly launched Android Design website.
  3. Roboto is commonly used in user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) design.
  4. Roboto have an almost perfect spacing and have a clear appearance.
  5. Roboto is specifically designed for use on screen and has been designed to balance content density with reading comfort.
